# 🔥 DailyYou — Habit Tracker

A personal habit tracker built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
No app to install. No account to create. Just open the link and start
building your streaks.

## What it does

- Add habits with categories — health, hobby, work, mindfulness
- Tap once a day to check in and keep your streak alive
- Watch your flame grow as your streak gets longer 
- See your last 7 days at a glance with a weekly dot grid
- Get a gentle nudge — not a guilt trip — if you miss a day
- Know exactly how many hours are left today to check in

## Why I built this

Life gets busy. Yoga, walking, drawing, drinking water — the things
that make us feel good are usually the first to slip. This app is
for the days when you only have one minute. One tap. That's enough
to keep the streak alive.

## Your data is yours

Everything is saved in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent
to any server. Each person who opens the link gets their own private
tracker on their own device.

## Built with

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript
- localStorage (no backend needed)

## How to use

Just open the link → [**Launch the app**]([https://preethi-karingu.github.io/Habit_Tracker/])

Or download `index.html` and open it in any browser on any device.
